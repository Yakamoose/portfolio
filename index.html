Happy Hour Now

Happy Hour Now streamlines the process of trying to find a happy hour to go to. Enter your location and the app will immediately return a list of happy hours, CURRENTLY HAPPENING AT THE TIME OF SEARCH, with 1-click links to menus and personalized Google Maps directions,don't waste your time searching through results that don't apply to you. Only available for West LA (for now).

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Google Maps API, Node.js, Express, Mocha

7 MILLION PAINTINGS

Inspired by Brian Eno, this app searches through a library of curated computer generated composite images and compounds them together to create never before seen dynamic pieces of art to view AND save. You can use this app like a visualizer while listening to music, or save your favorite pieces and use them for things like business cards, logos, and tee shirts, the possibilities are endless.

Modern Romance

Modern Romance is a spontaneous flower delivery service. What that means is that you choose your flower bouquet to deliver, and then choose the delivery window you want the flowers to be delivered in (2 weeks - 1 year). Then at a random time in your window your delivery will take place, thus making you the spontaneous and thoughtful person you are.

HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, Node.js, Express, Mocha
